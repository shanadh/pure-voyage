Day by Day Plan

Your Lakshadweep Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival & Island Exploration

Airport Pickup & Beach Sunset Walk

Arrive at Agatti Airport where our representative will welcome you and transfer you to your accommodation. After check-in and briefing, explore the local wonders including Western Jetty, pristine Anthan Beach, and Lagoon Beach. Enjoy a peaceful Agatti Village walk in the evening, followed by dinner.

  • Airport Pickup & Hotel Check-in
  • Visit Western Jetty & Anthan Beach
  • Lagoon Beach & Agatti Village Walk Dinner
  • Overnight stay at Agatti
Day 2

Thrilling Water Activities

Explore The Turquoise Marine Life

Indulge in an action-packed day of water sports in Agatti's pristine lagoon waters. Dive deep or paddle around to experience the rich marine ecology, green sea turtles, and colorful corals under professional guidance.

  • Snorkelling: Explore the vibrant underwater world
  • Kayaking: Paddle through crystal clear waters
  • Scuba Diving: Dive into another world (Optional)
Day 3

Let's Explore Kalpitty Island

Glass Boat Ride Excursion

Take a boat excursion to the gorgeous Kalpitty Island. Board a specialized glass-bottom boat to peek directly into the marine ecosystem, viewing exotic corals and tropical fishes comfortably without getting wet. Return to Agatti for your farewell dinner.

  • Boat Transfer to Kalpitty Island
  • Glass boatride: Experience marine life through glass bottom
  • Dinner and Overnight stay at Agatti
Day 4

Departure & Farewell

Journey Home With Memories

Savor your final tropical breakfast at the accommodation. Complete your check-out process, pack your bags, and proceed to Agatti Airport for your departure flight back home.

  • Morning Checkout after breakfast
  • Direct Transfer to Agatti Airport
Day 1

Arrival & Island Sightseeing

Airport Pickup & Cultural Exploration

Upon arrival at Agatti Airport, our team will receive you and provide road transfer to your room. After lunch, proceed for half-day island sightseeing to experience the traditions, customs, and culture of the local people of Lakshadweep.

  • Road transfer from Agatti Airport to room
  • Half-day Agatti island sightseeing
  • Local customs & culture immersion
Day 2

Dolphins & Fishing Excursion

Open Sea Excursion

After breakfast, set sail into the open sea for dolphin watching and fishing. Savor a hot lunch provided directly on the boat. Return to Agatti Island in the evening, enjoy hot tea, and relax during your overnight stay.

  • Dolphin spotting cruise & open sea fishing
  • Boat-deck lunch & evening refreshments
  • Return to Agatti for overnight stay
Day 3

Kalpitty Island Sunset Excursion

Optional Water Sports & Sunset Views

Savor breakfast at the hotel, then choose to participate in optional water sports activities. After lunch, enjoy a 45-minute scenic boat transfer to the uninhabited Kalpitty Island. Spend the afternoon taking in the incredible sunset views, then return to Agatti for your final overnight stay.

  • Morning optional water sports
  • Boat transfer to uninhabited Kalpitty Island (45 min)
  • Golden Hour sunset watching & Return to Agatti
Day 4

Departure

Sweet Memories of Lakshadweep

Enjoy your final breakfast in the island paradise. Complete checkout procedures, gather your bags, and transfer to Agatti Airport for your departure flight back home, carrying sweet memories of Lakshadweep.

  • Checkout after morning breakfast
  • Direct road transfer to Agatti Airport
